01 — About

I've been doing this a while.

I started in music, moved into code early, and later moved countries to build a better future. I do my best work when things are unclear, high-pressure, or half-broken. I don't waste time circling problems. I get in, figure out what actually matters, and start fixing.

Right now, most of my work runs through AI. I've built a personal ops setup that lets me go from a single prompt to a working app in under an hour. I use it to automate my workflows, cut out busywork, and ship faster than people expect. I pay attention to which tools are actually useful and skip the rest. AI is not a gimmick for me. It's how I build.

I'm also a Game Designer and Unity developer. I break games down constantly, looking at what works, what feels cheap, and which small design choices quietly shape the whole experience. Feel and systems are what I care about most. I keep half my life in spreadsheets. Games included.

Music is still in the foundation. I play acoustic gigs with my brother and perform in a System of a Down tribute band. Live performance taught me how to hold a room and read an audience, and that shows up in everything I make. I still produce on SoundCloud when the mood hits.
