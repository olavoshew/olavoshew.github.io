01 — About

Coding since 2010.
Playing since age 4.

I'm a developer who thinks in rhythm. Over a decade of building apps and games gave me a toolkit that goes beyond code — audio design, image and video editing, team leadership, and a deep instinct for how interactive experiences should feel.

Music came first. Playing instruments since I was four gave me an ear for timing, dynamics, and composition that carries into everything I build. When I'm not shipping apps, I'm producing tracks on SoundCloud.

I specialize in augmented reality and immersive mobile experiences — the kind of work where technology, craft, and a little bit of magic overlap.
